Ethiopia - Kebele

Experience Kebele, an exceptional coffee from Worka Sakaro Washing Station in the Gedio Zone. Cultivated by 400 smallholder farmers at 2,000-2,200 meters above sea level, under the shade of Birbira, Corsica Africana, and Ensete Ventricosum trees.Ripe cherries are carefully handpicked and dried on raised beds for 18-21 days. This lot showcases innovative anaerobic processing. After floating, cherries ferment in special bags with carbon dioxide and nitrogen gas for 3-7 days. Only when they reach the perfect pH and color, they're transferred to drying tables. Kebele embodies the harmonious balance between tradition and experimentation, resulting in an exquisite symphony of flavors. Savor the story of passion and dedication in each cup, crafted by nature and the skilled farmers in collaboration with the Worka Sakaro Washing Station.
